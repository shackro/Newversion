How do I create an account? +

Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in your details (name, email, phone), verify your email, and complete the KYC verification process. You'll need to provide valid identification documents.

Tip: Use a valid email address and phone number as you'll need to verify both.

What documents do I need for verification? +

You'll need:

  • Government-issued ID (Passport, National ID, or Driver's License)
  • Recent utility bill or bank statement (proof of address)
  • Recent passport-sized photograph

Important: All documents must be clear, valid, and show your full name matching your registration details.

How long does verification take? +

Typically 24-48 hours. We review all documents manually to ensure security and compliance. You'll receive an email notification once your account is verified.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept multiple payment methods:

  • M-Pesa: Instant deposits for Kenyan users
  • Bank Transfer: Local and international bank transfers
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express
  • Mobile Money: For users in supported countries
How long do withdrawals take? +

Withdrawal processing times:

  • M-Pesa: 1-2 hours during business hours
    • Bank Transfer: 24-48 hours
  • Weekends/Holidays: May experience delays

Note: First withdrawals may take longer due to security verification.

Are there any fees for deposits or withdrawals? +

Deposits are free. Withdrawal fees vary:

  • M-Pesa: 1% (minimum KSh 30)
  • Bank Transfer: 2% (minimum $10)
  • Card Withdrawals: 3% (minimum $15)

Important: Network fees may apply depending on your bank or mobile money provider.

üîí Security & Safety
How secure is my money and data? +

We implement multiple security layers:

  • Bank-Level Encryption: 256-bit SSL encryption for all data
  • Cold Storage: 95% of funds stored offline
  • 2FA: Optional two-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: Monthly security audits by third parties
  • Insurance: Funds insured against platform failure
What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access? +

Immediately:

  1. Change your password
  2. Enable 2FA if not already active
  3. Contact support@pesaprime.com
  4. Freeze your account through security settings
  5. Review recent transactions

Important: We monitor accounts 24/7 for suspicious activity and will notify you of any detected anomalies.
